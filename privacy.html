Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2023

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at TrueWind Canadian Marine Weather by TideByte Apps ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

What Information We Collect

We collect diagnostic crash reports and anonymous identifiers to help improve the stability and performance of our app. These reports do not include any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the diagnostic crash reports exclusively for troubleshooting purposes and to enhance the app's overall performance and reliability. Anonymous identifiers are collected to deliver notifications and other app functionalities.

Will Your Information be Shared with Anyone?

No, we do not share, sell, rent or trade the crash report data or anonymous identifiers with third parties.

How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We only keep diagnostic crash reports and anonymous identifiers for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes set out in this privacy policy, generally for the duration of the troubleshooting process and to provide ongoing app functionalities.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact us.
